About

I'm an ex-public finance professional turned full stack web developer. Having always been curious about technology, I learned the ropes of Ruby, Rails, Javascript, HTML, CSS and Git via the Bitmaker Labs' immersive web development bootcamp. I also hold a masters degree from the London School of Economics and an undergrad degree from the University of Toronto.

I love experimenting and making cool things, on and off the internet. In my spare time, you can find me hacking away on civic tech projects, organizing a Rails Girls TO event, or lifting things at a crossfit gym.
